The ingredients:

  • 100 g. flour
  • 10 g. sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • salt, pepper

The Procedure:

  • Mix dry ingredients thoroughly.
  • Pour in wet ingredients.
  • Mix for 10 minutes.
  • Bake for one hour at 300 degrees.
